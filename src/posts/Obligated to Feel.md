I have changed, I don't know why but still. 



I think I stopped caring. So, has the world you know. 



As someone who enjoyed and cared about future,I have stopped. It's weird we were excited about everything every music release every tech upgrade every day. But thinking about it, today the future seems so bleak that we don't think about.



I have given up. We look back. There has been a analog change if you have seen, people are wearing wired earbuds, buying CDs , downloading movies. It's fun to see that the culture is passed down by objects not just bytes you know. 



There is a sense to feel to be there but still.I have to responsible for things as someone who has everything. There are things that I care about, responsibilities I have, people I love, Ideas that once inspired me. Still I don't feel obligated to it. It might make me a bad person but I think the idea to feel is weird you know, If I have already given up on future, if we have already given up on future why feel. I know it makes a difference. I know it can change the world or at least the small world around me.Yet. 



The only thing I feel is the obligation to feel.
